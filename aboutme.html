About Me

Transforming Health, One Step at a Time

As a Registered Physiotherapist with CORU, Ireland's regulatory body for Healthcare Professionals, I am dedicated to helping individuals from all walks of life—whether athletes or those facing everyday challenges—regain their strength and well-being. Based in Castletroy, Co. Limerick, I work in private practice, offering personalized care to address issues affecting performance, work, and daily activities. I also provide services at Sports & Spinal Physiotherapy in Clonmel, Co. Tipperary, where we have a fully equipped gym to support client assessment and rehabilitation.

Currently, I serve as the physiotherapist for the Waterford Senior Footballers, and have had the privilege of working with the Tipperary U20 Football team for two years. As a competitive powerlifter with the Irish Powerlifting Federation, I have a deep understanding of how injuries can affect not just physical performance, but also mental resilience. My mission is to guide clients through recovery, build strength, and help them return to doing what they love with confidence.

If you have any questions or would just like to say hello, feel free to reach out. I'm always happy to connect and discuss how we can work together to help you achieve your goals!
