Services

If you're recovering from an injury, preparing for surgery, or aiming to prevent future issues, I provide a wide range of physiotherapy services designed to support your health and well-being. Every treatment plan is personalized to help you regain mobility, build strength, and stay active—so you can move with confidence and get back to doing what you love.

  • Physical Therapy
  • Pre-Operative Physio & Prehab
  • Post-Operative Physio & Rehabilitation
  • Pitchside Physio & First-Aid
  • Swedish Massage, Deep Tissue Massage, Sports Massage
  • Events: Pre-Event & Post-Event Treatments
  • Corporate (On-Site) Treatments
  • House Calls: Limerick City
  • Private Patients in Retirement Homes
  • Fall Prevention in Elderly Patients

I treat a wide range of conditions that can impact your daily life and performance. Each treatment plan is tailored to address your specific needs, helping you reduce pain, restore function, and improve overall movement—so you can stay active and feel your best.

  • Back & Neck Pain (including Work-Related Pain and Tension Headaches)
  • Shoulder Conditions (Frozen Shoulder, Shoulder Bursitis, Thoracic Outlet Syndrome)
  • Elbow & Wrist Injuries (Tennis Elbow, Golfer’s Elbow, Carpal Tunnel Syndrome, Tenosynovitis)
  • Hip & Knee Issues (Hip Pain, Runner’s Knee, Patellar Tendonitis, Osteoarthritis Management & Exercise Prescription)
  • Ankle & Foot Problems (Recurrent Ankle Sprain, Achilles Tendonitis, Ligament Injury)
  • Sciatic & Nerve-Related Pain (Sciatica, Nerve Entrapment, Intervertebral Disc Bulge, Piriformis Syndrome)
  • Sports Injuries & Rehabilitation (ACL Sprain & Rehabilitation, Sports Injuries, Facet Joint Syndrome)
  • Bone Health & Aging (Osteoporosis, Fall Prevention in Elderly Patients)

Not sure which treatment is right for you? Get in touch, and let’s discuss your needs.
